About Me

Hi, I'm
Jorja.

I’m a Mid-Canterbury-based photographer focused on capturing sport, people, and real-life moments as they unfold. My style is natural, energetic, and story-driven — aiming to show the intensity, emotion, and personality behind every shot, so your photos feel like you, just in the moment.

Whether it’s a game day on the sideline, a training session in motion, or capturing the people and stories behind your business, I show up ready to document real moments as they happen — fast, honest, and full of energy.
